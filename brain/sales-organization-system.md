## Organization Framework

### 1. Daily Sales Routine
**Morning (8am):**
- Receive automated morning brief
- Review weather, tasks, news, tips
- Plan the day's priorities

**Throughout Day:**
- Log every client interaction immediately
- Update pipeline after each call/email
- Take notes in standardized format

**Evening (before bed):**
- Review what got done
- Plan tomorrow's top 3 priorities
- Update todo.md

**Night (11pm - automated):**
- AI builds tools and improvements while you sleep

### 2. Pipeline Management
**Lead Stages:**
1. Cold → First contact
2. Warm → Interested, needs nurturing
3. Hot → Ready to close
4. Won → Closed deal
5. Lost → Document why

**Update frequency:** After every interaction

### 3. Follow-Up System
**Never let a lead go cold:**
- Set follow-up dates for every contact
- Use automated reminders
- Track: Last contact, next action, notes

### 4. Email Organization
**Folders:**
- Inbox Zero approach
- Action Required
- Waiting For Response
- Reference/Archive

**Templates:**
- Introduction email
- Follow-up (1st, 2nd, 3rd)
- Proposal sent
- Thank you after meeting
- Closing email

### 5. Time Blocking
**Block your calendar:**
- 9-11am: Outbound calls
- 11am-1pm: Emails & admin
- 2-4pm: Meetings/demos
- 4-5pm: Pipeline updates & planning
