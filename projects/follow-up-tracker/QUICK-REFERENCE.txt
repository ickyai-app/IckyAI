================================================================================
  COMMON QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: How often should I log activities?
A: After every call/email/visit. Takes 1 minute.

Q: What status values should I use?
A: NEW, QUALIFIED, DEMO COMPLETED, AWAITING RESPONSE, QUOTE SENT, 
   NEGOTIATING, STALLED, NO RESPONSE, CLOSED

Q: What if my deal values are in ‚Ç¨ with comma thousands separator?
A: Remove them: 25000 (not 25.000 or ‚Ç¨25,000)

Q: How do I know if I'm doing well?
A: Look for trends: Pipeline growing? Activities increasing? 
   Closure rate improving? Cycle time shrinking?

Q: Can I change the system?
A: Yes! It's all yours. CSV files are standard format, scripts are editable.

Q: What if I miss logging an activity?
A: Add it later when you remember. Better late than never.
